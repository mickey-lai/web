Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I start my 7-day free trial? +
Simply click the "7-Day Free Trial" button in the top navigation or on any pricing page. You'll need to create an account with your email address. No credit card is required for the trial, and you'll have full access to all premium features for 7 days.
What's included in the free trial? +
The free trial includes access to all premium features: advanced stock screener with 500+ filters, portfolio analysis tools, backtesting engine, guru tracking, real-time data, custom alerts, and API access. You can also export data and use all professional tools without any limitations.
How accurate is your financial data? +
We source our data from multiple tier-1 financial data providers including Reuters, Bloomberg, and S&P Global. Our data is updated in real-time during market hours and undergoes rigorous quality checks. We maintain 99.9% data accuracy and provide timestamps for all data points.
Can I import my existing portfolio? +
Yes! You can import your portfolio in several ways: upload a CSV file with your holdings, connect directly to supported brokers (TD Ameritrade, E*TRADE, Schwab), or manually enter your positions. We support stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, and options across multiple exchanges.
How does the stock screener work? +
Our stock screener allows you to filter stocks based on 500+ criteria including fundamental metrics (P/E, revenue growth, debt levels), technical indicators (RSI, moving averages, volume), and custom combinations. You can save screens, set up alerts, and even backtest your screening strategies to see historical performance.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. You can update your payment method anytime in your account settings.
Is there an API available? +
Yes, we offer a comprehensive RESTful API for premium users. You can access real-time and historical stock data, screening results, portfolio analytics, and more. The API includes detailed documentation, code examples in multiple languages, and generous rate limits. Perfect for algorithmic trading and custom applications.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers.
