About GuruFocus

GuruFocus is a leading investment research platform that empowers both professional and individual investors with advanced screening tools, comprehensive market analysis, and data-driven insights. Founded in 2018, we've revolutionized how investors discover opportunities and make informed decisions.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with institutional-grade research capabilities, offering over 500 screening filters, real-time backtesting, portfolio analysis, and expert guru tracking. We serve over 15,000 active users worldwide, from individual retail investors to professional fund managers.

At GuruFocus, we believe that every investor deserves access to professional-grade tools and insights. Our mission is to democratize investment research by making sophisticated analysis accessible, affordable, and actionable for everyone.

Key Differentiators

What sets GuruFocus apart is our comprehensive approach to investment research. Unlike competitors who focus on single aspects of analysis, we provide an all-in-one platform that covers screening, backtesting, portfolio optimization, and market intelligence in one unified interface.
